- `pmsec` is one CLI implemented in `node/`, `python/`, `bash/`, and `powershell/`.
- User-visible behavior stays aligned across ports. Change all affected ports together.
- `conformance/` is the contract for cross-port behavior.
- `powershell/` is the only intentional exception: Windows-only, WSL-aware, and may expose Windows-scope-only flags/output.
